\section{Research Statement (Summary)}
My research advances the theory and practice of robust machine learning for cyber-physical systems, with emphasis on sample-efficient reinforcement learning, formal verification of neural controllers, and safety guarantees for autonomous systems. I develop methods that bridge control theory and statistical learning, targeting applications in power grid control, robotics, and autonomous vehicles.
